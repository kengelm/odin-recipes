Potato Chip Cookies Recipe

Description

Whenever I'm having a bad day, I want nothing more to sit down and eat a delicious, warm chocolate chip cookie. But I've been having a lot of bad days, so I have grown tired of my go-to cookie recipe. Enter the potato chip. It's the perfect savory snack to rescue the chocolate chip cookie from going stale by adding a unique spin its signature soft texture. This recipes is from Chef John's website Food Wishes.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Unsalted butter (2 sticks)
  • White sugar
  • Light brown sugar
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Potato chips
  • Dark chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Whisk together dry ingredients.
  2. Beat together wet ingredients.
  3. Add potato and chocolate chips to wet ingredients.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Chill dough for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  7. Scoop dough onto baking sheet and spread evenly.
  8. Bake for about 12 minutes.
  9. Let sit for 5 minutes before moving to a wire rack to cool completely.
